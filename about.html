Our History

Founded in 2010 by parents and educators, Little Bird Toys started as a small workshop crafting wooden toys from locally sourced materials. It has since grown into a respected brand known for quality and sustainability.

Our Commitment

The Commitment we have towards our customers is just as strong as the commitment we have towards our employees and sustainability.

  • Materials: Sustainably sourced wood, non-toxic paints.
  • Manufacturing: Energy-efficient, minimal waste.
  • Safety: Rigorous testing, international standards compliance.
  • Packaging: Minimal, recyclable materials.
  • Community: Supports local economies and educational initiatives.

Our Values

  • Quality: High standards of craftsmanship and safety.
  • Sustainability: Eco-friendly materials and practices.
  • Education: Toys that stimulate learning and creativity.
  • Community: Support for local artisans and fair trade practices.
